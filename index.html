Why building it stalls
Homegrown systems plateau because context is the hard problem.

A shared, evolving, decision-aware understanding of the org is an ongoing systems challenge — not a feature you bolt on. It's called context engineering. It's the wall every homegrown build hits, and it's the one we've spent our build solving.
